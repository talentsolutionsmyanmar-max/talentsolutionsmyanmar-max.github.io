08 / Questions

Fair questions. Straight answers.

Is ReferTRM really free for job seekers?+
Yes. We earn from employer placement fees, not from candidates. You will never be charged.
How does the referral commission work?+
When someone you refer gets hired, the employer pays a placement fee. You receive 70–85% of that fee depending on your tier.
What is Maya?+
Maya is our AI career advisor. She answers career questions in Myanmar and English, 24/7.
Do I need to pay for courses?+
All 190+ courses are free at Foundation level. Premium tiers add advanced features like AI mentoring.

We never sell your data. Licensed by Ministry of Labour, License No. 211/2024. Your information is encrypted and protected.
