Climb by opening doors.

Four bands. Each gate opens on evidence — a verified placement outcome — never on activity, never on time served. You move when the record says you moved.

Referrer

Gate: verified placements
R3

BD Freelancer

You work requisitions end to end with the platform behind you — client truth, candidate care, clean documentation.

Gate: sustained outcomes + RTC
R4

Your own book

A portable record of clients and placements that is yours. The ladder ends where your practice begins.

The book is yours
Evidence-backed, not activity-backed. A placement counts when the candidate starts and the client confirms — the P1 verification standard. Shares, clicks and log-ins never open a gate.

06 · Certificate — the RTC register

The RTC register.

Complete RTC M1–M4 and hold a first verified placement, and your name enters the Recruiter Training Certificate register. The RTC certifies that you trained and produced real outcomes on this platform. It is a training credential — it is not an agency licence, and it does not make anyone a broker.

·Issued on evidence: modules complete, first verified placement on record.
·Revocable on conduct: candidate harm or dishonest referral ends the entry.
·Named plainly: a certificate of training and outcomes — nothing more claimed, nothing less.
