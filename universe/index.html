The Mission: 100K in Year One

The target this entire universe is built to hit: 100,000 registered users from Myanmar by month 12–18 — then 1,000,000 by years 3–5, with 10,000 daily visitors as the permanent floor. Not LinkedIn-scale, not Facebook-scale — Myanmar's verified network scale. The health metric underneath it all is TAW (Truth-Active Weekly): people who did something real in the last 7 days. All figures shown are illustrative trajectory, not reported metrics.

The four quarters of year one

Q1 · MONTHS 1–3 · FOUNDATION
Trust enters the feed → ~5K

Engine to full cadence (14+ posts/week), trust strip becomes recognizable, Viber/Telegram rooms alive with human answers, Weekly Roundup establishes rhythm. Campaigns: VERIFIED soft-launch. Win condition: people start asking "is it verified?" back to us in chat.

Q2 · MONTHS 4–6 · MOMENTUM
Loops open → ~25K cumulative

First Job, Real Salary + Walk-In Weekend campaigns fire, TikTok cuts scale, X threads build authority, referral loop opens with first server-verified Handoff proofs. Win condition: referral-driven signups become visible in the funnel — the flywheel's first full turn.

Q3 · MONTHS 7–9 · COMPOUNDING
Stories become supply → ~60K cumulative

The Climb docuseries EP1–3 lands on YouTube and feeds 12–18 TikTok cuts, search territory pages go live (via CTO→Codex), employer spotlights widen verified inventory. Win condition: content is now partially made from user outcomes — the engine feeds itself.

Q4 · MONTHS 10–12 · SCALE
Habit at scale → 100K

All four campaigns running, cadence peaks at 21+ posts/week, community is a weekly ritual, roundup is an institution. Months 12–18: referrals compound past linear content growth. Win condition: 100,000 registered — and the machine that made them is stronger than the number.

Quarterly markers are illustrative pacing, not promises — the Performance seat owns real targets and re-forecasts monthly. The shape is the strategy: slow trust first, compounding later.

Motion grammar

Word-groups appear at reading pace, never word-by-word karaoke · holds ≥1.5s · one gold accent moment per video (the trust lock-on) · cuts on beat but calm · captions always on · reduced-motion respected on all interactive surfaces.

Footage law

Real workplaces only with employer consent on file · real people only with signed consent · no stock-office fakery (that's scam grammar) · dramatizations are labeled as dramatizations · dignity framing passes the Mother Test before export.

Voice Law

How ReferTRM sounds everywhere — and the words that are banned because they sound like the enemy.

We sound like

  • A straight-talking friend who happens to have the receipts. Warm, direct, concrete.
  • Numbers before adjectives. "MMK 350,000" beats "great salary" every time.
  • Short sentences. If a line needs two breaths, it's two lines.
  • Protective, never alarmist. We arm people against scams; we don't terrorize them.

Banned vocabulary — scam grammar

attractive salary urgent hiring limited slots DM your CV guaranteed income earn from your phone a leading company act now amazing opportunity escape poverty

Any asset containing a banned phrase fails brand lint automatically. No exceptions, no "but it performs."
